Dear Asmita

Last Edited: 18 June 2026 - 12:00AM

Kal raat jab main sone ja raha tha aur tumhari photo dekh raha tha, tab pata nahi kyun ye sab baatein mere mann mein aa rahi thi. Shayad isliye kyunki kuch baatein aisi hoti hain jo hum keh nahi paate, bas mehsoos karte rehte hain.

Kabhi-kabhi mujhe lagta hai ki shayad main tumhe kuch cheezon ke liye zyada force karta hoon. Jab main baar-baar baat karne ko kehta hoon ya tumhara intezar karta hoon, to mujhe darr lagta hai ki kahin main tum par pressure to nahi daal raha. Main nahi chahta ki tum kabhi bhi meri wajah se uncomfortable ya pressured feel karo.

Lekin ek baat jo shayad main tumhe utni baar nahi batata, woh ye hai ki main tumhari har koshish ko notice karta hoon. Main jaanta hoon ki ghar par rehkar bhi tumhare paas apni responsibilities hoti hain, apne kaam hote hain aur aas pass log hote hai , phir bhi tum mere liye waqt nikal leti ho. Main us baat ki dil se respect karta hoon.

Aur kabhi-kabhi mujhe lagta hai ki tum sochti hogi ki ye kitna free rehta hai, main jab bhi message karun iska reply turant aa jaata hai. 😅

Sach kahun to uska reason bas itna hai ki tum meri priority ho. Tumhara message aata hai to chahe main kitna bhi busy kyu na hoon, mera dhyaan sabse pehle usi par chala jaata hai.

Kabhi-kabhi college wale din yaad aate hain. Tab sab kuch kitna simple lagta tha. Hum zyada baat kar paate the, zyada waqt saath bita paate the. Shayad isi wajah se aaj bhi main un chhoti-chhoti baaton ko yaad karta hoon.

Ye letter kisi shikayat ke liye nahi hai, na kisi complain ke liye. Bas dil mein jo chal raha tha, woh tumse share karna tha. Aur shayad isliye bhi, kyunki tum meri zindagi ke un logon mein se ho jinke saath main apne dil ki baat bina kisi dikhave ke kar sakta hoon.

Bas itna hi... ❤️

Yours,
Suman ❤️
